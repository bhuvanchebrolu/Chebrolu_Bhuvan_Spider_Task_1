📘 Game Instructions

  1. Objective: Connect 4 discs in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
  2. Start: Each player gets 4 discs.
  3. Blocking: Before a move, opponent blocks one column.
  4. Placement: Drop your disc in any unblocked column.
  5. Victory: First to align 4 discs wins.
  6. Draw: Happens when board is full or both run out of discs.
  7. Timer: 30 seconds per move. Timeout = opponent wins.
  8. Undo: Revert your last move or block.
  9. Leaderboard: Tracks wins and draws for both players.
  10. Theme & Pause: Toggle dark/light mode, pause & resume anytime.
